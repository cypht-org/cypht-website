    <section id="advanced-features" class="advanced-features-section container mt-5">
        <h2 class='text-center'>Advanced Features for Complete Management</h2>
        <p class="lead text-center mb-5">Discover the power and flexibility of Cypht.</p>

        <div class="row">
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-inbox"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Unified Inbox</h4>
                        <p>Combined inbox, unread, sent, and flagged message views for <b>all your E-mail accounts </b>(and RSS feeds), as well as standard E-mail client folder navigation.</p>
                        
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-left">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-users-cog"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Flexible Profiles</h4>
                        <p>Flexible profiles to combined IMAP accounts with SMTP accounts and <b>setup signatures</b> and reply to details.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-exchange-alt"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Move or copy emails from one account to another</h4>
                        <p>Easily transfer or duplicate emails <b>from one account to another</b>, giving you complete flexibility in organizing your communications.</p>
                        
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-left">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-pen-fancy"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Versatile Composition</h4>
                        <p>Compose messages in <b>plain text, HTML, or Markdown</b> (markdown formatted messages are converted to HTML before being sent)</p>
                        
                        
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-filter"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Server Side Sieve Filtering</h4>
                        <p>Automatically organize your inbox with server-side Sieve filtering, your emails are sorted <b>even when you are offline</b>.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-left">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-search"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Advanced Multi-Account Search</h4>
                        <p>Search simultaneously in <b>all your email accounts and RSS feeds</b> through a simple and intuitive form, to quickly find information.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-language"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Simplified Translation</h4>
                        <p>Enjoy a simple interface translation system based on PHP arrays, without .po files or gettext. <b>Support for right-to-left languages</b>.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-left">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-cubes"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Rich Module Sets</h4>
                        <p>A set of modules for IMAP, SMTP, LDAP/local contacts, WordPress, GitHub, and much more! Check out our <a href="URL_VERS_PAGE_MODULES" target="_blank">Modules page</a> for a complete list.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-database"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Flexible Data Storage</h4>
                        <p>User sessions and data can be stored in <b>any PDO compatible database</b> or directly in flat files on the server.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-left">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-fingerprint"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Adaptable Authentication</h4>
                        <p>Flexible authentication supporting IMAP, LDAP, included database schema, popular email providers, domain-based autodiscovery, or customize it with the site's modular set.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6" data-aos="fade-right">
                <div class="advanced-feature-card">
                    <i class="fas fa-puzzle-piece"></i>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Personalization Without Disruption</h4>
                        <p>Sessions and authentication can be customized <b>without breaking existing modules</b>, thanks to the site's module set, ensuring great scalability.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>
